Wattpad Next (Beta)

Support From Your Audience

Being connected to your readers is one of the most satisfying and rewarding parts of Wattpad. With Wattpad Next Beta, your audience can also reward you with monetary support. Wattpad Next Beta allows readers to support selected authors by purchasing access to entire stories or paying to unlock portions as they go.

How Does Wattpad Next Beta Work?

For full information about the program, you can check out its website here.

For writers though, we offer a coaching program behind the scenes to assist all the authors who join. We identify content that we want to add, contact the writer, and then onboard them to our own community, where they will gain access to additional resources and the support of a coach.
