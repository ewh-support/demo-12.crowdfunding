Writer Programs & Opportunities

Writers like you are the heartbeat of the Wattpad community. We work tirelessly to create programs to support creativity and growth, and opportunities to help writers connect their stories with new audiences. From online programs like The Wattys to IRL events like WattCon, there’s something for everyone.

The Wattys

Recognizing greatness in storytelling across the Wattpad universe.

Wattpad Books

Publishing diverse, talented voices from the Wattpad community.

Wattpad Next (Beta)

Wattpad Picks

WattCon

Two days. Hundreds of Wattpad creators. Your stories.

Wattpad Stars

Dedicated support for the best and brightest Wattpad writers.

Wattpad Studios

Propelling original Wattpad stories into the entertainment world.

Contests

Sparking storytelling movements through exciting partnerships, exposure and prizing.

Wattpad Futures

Monetize your stories through in-story ads.
