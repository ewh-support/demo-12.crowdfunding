Inspire SciFi

22.9K 357 462
by ScienceFiction
    Prompts of various sorts are an excellent way to inspire a new story, or give your brain a jolt in the ideas department. Many writers also use them to add a new dimension or direction to an existing story. 
  
    4
  
  


    
                    
    We here at the ScienceFiction account love the idea that a single, simple prompt can inspire anything from a vignette to a story spanning the cosmos. 
  
    +
  
  


    
                    
    Whilst we have many prompt ideas swimming around in the vortex of mnemonic phrases, we would like to open the floor to our wonderful followers and let our fellow SciFi fanatics to have a chance at having their prompt used in one of our contests.
  
    3
  
  


    
                    
    Here's what we have in mind: using the comments section below, please give details of your prompt. Prompts can be absolutely anything. For our contests however, please bear in mind that they must tie in with Science Fiction in some way. 
  
    +
  
  


    
                    
    Should we decide to use your prompt, we will dedicate the challenge to you.
  
    +
  
  


    
                    
    And, if you as a writer use a prompt you find within these pages, feel free to dedicate your story to the Wattpadder who supplied the prompt that inspired you. 
  
    +
  
  


    
                    
    We look forward to seeing your ideas and comments.
